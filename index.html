About Video Dazed

Video Dazed is a digital autoethnography that examines youth media producers within the skateboarding subculture. Inspired by my own educational experiences growing up, I investigate how young skateboarders interact with technology and media and how it could fit into the broader discussion of reimagining educational environments, specifically the Connected Learning framework. This project employs new media tools to accurately document and portray three groups of youthful skateboarders that utilize media and technology in a truly rich and unique way. By leveraging Connected Learning, this study systematically aligns skateboarding and skateboard media production within the contexts and principles presented in the framework.
